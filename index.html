About

Hello! I am Jinseok Kim, a master's student at Big Data AI Laboratory in SNU, advised by Professor Sungzoon Cho. My research focuses on leveraging the interaction between Large Language Models (LLMs) and Search Systems to address challenges in information retrieval and real-world applications. More broadly, I am also interested in natural language understanding and its applications.
